Introduction

The Curse of Corvus Farm is a 5th Edition compatible adventure designed for a party of four level 3 characters. In this adventure, your players will uncover the tragic story of Corvus Greaves, a farmer cursed to walk the earth as a scarecrow. The players will journey through the haunted Greaves farm, navigate the eerie corn maze, and confront the Crow Folk that haunt Corvusâ€™s fields.

The adventure includes multiple hooks, opportunities for roleplaying, combat, and environmental challenges. It is designed to be modular, allowing you to customize the experience for your campaign or use it as a standalone one-shot adventure.
